Website Project Plan - Book Reviews Website

Project Subject

Subject: Book Reviews Website

Why this subject: Reading is one of my favourite hobbies, so I have deep personal investment in discussion of what people are reading and what genres are currently popular. Reading is also a widespread hobby that brings a number of very different people together, so providing a common place of discussion would be of interest to a large community of potential users.

Site Name

Example: Book Reviewers Forum

This name directly communicates the point of the webpage, to give, review and discuss information regarding book reviews.

Optional domain availability: BookReviewForum.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a forum in which people can review the general opinions of books that have been released, as well as get book recomendations for specific genres, see a list of what an author has written, and give their own opinions of books, genres or authors.

Planned Content

  • Book reviews
  • Book recomendations for specific genres
  • Information on the books being reviewed
  • Community forum to discuss books

Scenarios

  • What are good romance novels currently available?
  • Is the last book in the Inheritance Cycle any good?
  • Has the writer of Elantris written any other books?
